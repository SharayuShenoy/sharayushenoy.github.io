Ally: Productivity Assistant

Contributors: Dorian, Sharayu, Yiping, Yuqing
Course: Advanced Design of AI

This project was started in an attempt to answer the eternal question:

How might we improve productivity?

Limited with the ability to visualize a system that can be realized 5 years down the line, the team set off on a mission to envision a life made more productive(or better?) with AI.

Why?

People often struggle to manage their time and prioritize tasks especially when there are no hard deadlines. We noticed this frustration amongst our friends who were pursuing Ph.D. as struggled to get started on their thesis. As grad students, we also find ourselves weary to start a task that requires too much effort and ultimately end-up falling short on daylight. Unlike a regular 9-5 corporate job, the timings are flexible and which tends to make students more susceptible to procrastination.

So, will AI be a good solution?

In order to understand this and the problem better, we went through a sprint where we mapped our user frame, business frame, problem frame and customer-value fit.
Based on that, we deviced a research plan and followed an AI Design Sprint Cycle

All of these efforts culminated into the lifestory of ZOE!
