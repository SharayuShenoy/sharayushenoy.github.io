Build Your World

Contributors: Sharayu Shenoy, Elizabeth Tate, and Lu Jin
Course: Virtual Environments

Project Deliverables: Final Paper

Purpose and Importance

Simple and Calming Virtual Environments can still be used as a distraction to help calm people down during or after stressful situations.

we remembered watching Bob Ross on PBS as children and the calming effect it had on us. This project tried to emulate a similar feeling by allowing the user to build their own world using an array of nature elements.
